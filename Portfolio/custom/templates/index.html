Who is Behind All This?

Greetings and welcome to my photography portfolio. With a comprehensive span of 5 years in the field, I have ventured beyond my academic roots in civil engineering to fervently pursue my passion for photography. As a photographer, I specialize in the domains of portrait, product, and fashion photography, contributing a distinct vision and a refined skill set to each project. From skillfully revealing the true essence of individuals in exquisite portraits to adeptly showcasing the captivating allure of products and fashion, each captured image narrates a compelling story.

My professional journey led me to assume roles as both portrait and fashion photographer at VOLGA Studio, a venture that I proudly co-founded. Throughout my tenure, I also had the opportunity to explore an array of photography genres, encompassing disciplines like food and product photography. Recently, I have embarked on a new phase in my professional expedition, relocating to Canada. Fueled by enthusiasm, I am eager to immerse myself within a more distinguished and elevated setting, where growth and advancement take precedence. As I step into this fresh chapter, my commitment remains steadfast: a perpetual quest for refining my craft while nimbly adapting to the dynamic demands of the field.

I cordially invite you to explore my portfolio, a confluence where art harmonizes with photography through the lenses of portraiture, products, and fashion. I extend my heartfelt gratitude for joining me on this visually journey.
